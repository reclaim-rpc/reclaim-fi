When to use each

Flashbots Protect

Best for simplicity

Backed by the Flashbots team who pioneered MEV protection. Solid privacy guarantees and revert protection. Best if you want the most established name in the space and don't need active MEV rebates.

MEV Blocker

Best for maximum rebate percentage

Highest rebate rate at 90% of backrun value. Built by CoW Protocol with a proven OFA (Order Flow Auction) mechanism. Best if maximizing the raw rebate percentage is your priority.

Reclaim

Best for long-term ecosystem value

Active MEV capture (not just passive refunds), on-chain verifiable rebates, referral program, and a roadmap to DEX + wallet + builder. Best if you want a growing protocol that returns more value over time.
